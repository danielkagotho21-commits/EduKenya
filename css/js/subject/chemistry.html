Chemistry Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Atomic number of hydrogen → 1
  2. Symbol of sodium → Na
  3. pH of neutral solution → 7
  4. Define molecule → Two or more atoms chemically bonded
  5. State physical change → Ice melting
  6. State chemical change → Iron rusting
  7. Gas produced when acid reacts with metal → Hydrogen
  8. Unit of amount of substance → Mole
  9. Formula of water → H₂O
  10. Formula of carbon dioxide → CO₂
  11. Define mixture → Two or more substances not chemically combined
  12. Example of an acid → HCl
  13. Example of a base → NaOH
  14. Define solute → Substance dissolved in a solvent
  15. Define solvent → Substance in which solute dissolves
