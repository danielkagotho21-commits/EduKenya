Geography Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Longest river in Kenya → Tana River
  2. Highest mountain in Kenya → Mount Kenya
  3. Capital city of Kenya → Nairobi
  4. Equator passes through which Kenyan towns? → Nanyuki, Kisumu
  5. Major lake in Kenya → Lake Victoria
  6. Define climate → Average weather conditions of a place
  7. Example of a renewable resource → Water, solar energy
  8. Define population density → Number of people per unit area
  9. Major vegetation types in Kenya → Forest, grassland, savannah
  10. Define erosion → Wearing away of the earth’s surface
  11. Example of a non-renewable resource → Coal, petroleum
  12. Define plateau → Flat elevated land
  13. Define river basin → Area drained by a river and its tributaries
  14. Major ocean bordering Kenya → Indian Ocean
  15. Meaning of longitude → Distance east or west of the Prime Meridian
