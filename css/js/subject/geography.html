Geography Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Longest river in Kenya → Tana
  2. Highest mountain → Mount Kenya
  3. Capital → Nairobi
  4. Equator towns → Nanyuki, Kisumu
  5. Major lake → Victoria
  6. Define climate → Average weather
  7. Renewable resource → Water
  8. Population density → People per unit area
  9. Vegetation types → Forest, grassland
  10. Erosion → Wearing away of land
  11. Non-renewable → Coal
  12. Plateau → Flat elevated land
  13. River basin → Area drained by river
  14. Ocean bordering Kenya → Indian Ocean
  15. Longitude → Distance E/W of Prime Meridian
