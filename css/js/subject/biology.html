Biology Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Unit of life → Cell
  2. Define photosynthesis → Process by which plants make food using sunlight.
  3. Main organ for respiration → Lungs
  4. Function of red blood cells → Transport oxygen
  5. Part of plant that absorbs water → Roots
  6. Type of reproduction producing identical offspring → Asexual
  7. Define ecosystem → Interaction of organisms with their environment
  8. Unit of heredity → Gene
  9. Largest organ in human body → Skin
  10. Define habitat → Place where an organism lives
  11. Type of blood that carries oxygen → Red blood cells
  12. Define adaptation → Features that help an organism survive
  13. Example of a producer → Plant
  14. Example of a consumer → Human / Lion
  15. Define nutrition → Process of obtaining food and energy
