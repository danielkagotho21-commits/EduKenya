English Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Define a noun → A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.
  2. Define a verb → A word that shows action or state of being.
  3. Correct the sentence: "He go to school" → He goes to school.
  4. Identify the adjective: "The tall man is walking." → Tall
  5. Synonym of happy → Joyful / Glad
  6. Antonym of cold → Hot / Warm
  7. Write a sentence using the word "because" → I stayed home because it was raining.
  8. Correct punctuation: "where are you" → Where are you?
  9. Plural form of mouse → Mice
  10. Correct the tense: "She eat breakfast" → She eats breakfast.
  11. Define a pronoun → A word that replaces a noun.
  12. Identify the adverb: "He ran quickly." → Quickly
  13. Write past tense of run → Ran
  14. Give an example of a preposition → In, on, under, between
  15. Change to passive voice: "The cat chased the mouse." → The mouse was chased by the cat.
