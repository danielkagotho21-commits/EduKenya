English Revision

Sample Questions

  1. Define noun → Person, place, thing, idea
  2. Define verb → Action or state of being
  3. Correct: "He go to school" → He goes to school
  4. Identify adjective: "The tall man" → tall
  5. Synonym of happy → Joyful
  6. Antonym of cold → Hot
  7. Sentence with "because" → I stayed home because it rained
  8. Punctuation: "where are you" → Where are you?
  9. Plural of mouse → Mice
  10. Correct tense: "She eat breakfast" → She eats breakfast
  11. Define pronoun → Replaces a noun
  12. Identify adverb: "He ran quickly" → quickly
  13. Past tense of run → ran
  14. Example of preposition → in, on
  15. Passive: "The cat chased the mouse" → The mouse was chased by the cat
