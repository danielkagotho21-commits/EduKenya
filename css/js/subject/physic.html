Physics Revision

Sample Questions

  1. State Newton’s First Law → Inertia: A body remains at rest or uniform motion unless acted on by a force.
  2. Unit of force → Newton (N)
  3. Speed formula → Speed = Distance ÷ Time
  4. Acceleration formula → a = (v-u)/t
  5. Weight of a body formula → W = mg
  6. Energy unit → Joule (J)
  7. Define power → Power = Work ÷ Time
  8. Unit of pressure → Pascal (Pa)
  9. State Ohm’s law → V = IR
  10. Unit of electric charge → Coulomb (C)
  11. Define density → Density = Mass ÷ Volume
  12. Unit of frequency → Hertz (Hz)
  13. What is refraction? → Bending of light when it passes from one medium to another.
  14. What is reflection? → Bouncing back of light from a surface.
  15. State law of conservation of energy → Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed.
